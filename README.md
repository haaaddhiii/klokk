# Klokk — Minimalist Focus Timer

Klokk is a calm, minimal web-based clock and Pomodoro timer designed to help you stay focused without the clutter of traditional productivity apps. 

## ✨ Features

- **Minimalist Aesthetic:** A warm, neutral color palette designed to reduce digital eye strain.
- **Dual Modes:**
  - **Clock Mode:** A clean digital clock with a 12h/24h toggle and a subtle "breathing" indicator.
  - **Focus Mode:** A Pomodoro timer with a visual progress ring and session tracking.
- **Responsive & Optimized:** 
  - Works perfectly on desktop and mobile.
  - **New Landscape Mode:** Specifically optimized for mobile landscape orientation with side-by-side controls and dynamic centering when idle.
- **Smart Idle UI:** Controls automatically fade away during inactivity to provide a distraction-free experience.
- **Customizable:** Adjustable focus, short break, and long break durations.

- **HTML5** & **CSS3** (Vanilla CSS for maximum performance)
- **JavaScript** (Vanilla JS, no dependencies)
- **Google Fonts** (Jost & DM Mono)

## 📱 Mobile Experience

Klokk is designed to be a "companion screen." Prop your phone up in **landscape mode** on your desk to use it as a dedicated focus station. The UI will automatically adapt to place controls on the right for easy access, and center the timer when you're focusing.
